Learning HTML

Published on October 9, 2023

Learning HTML has been an exciting and enlightening experience for me. As a beginner in the world of web development, I'd like to share some of the highlights and challenges I've encountered so far.

One of the first things that struck me about HTML was its simplicity. The language is surprisingly easy to understand, with its use of tags enclosed in angle brackets. I quickly learned how to structure text, create headings, paragraphs, and links.

The wealth of learning resources available online played a pivotal role in my learning journey. Websites like Mozilla Developer Network (MDN) and W3Schools became my go-to references. Online tutorials and courses provided structured learning paths, helping me grasp HTML concepts progressively.
